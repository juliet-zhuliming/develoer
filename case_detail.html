English Education

APP introduction

English Education is a product for family English education,designed for Chinese children who are older than 6 years old. It turns Alpha 2 into your private English teacher. With specialists designing the course modules and combining them with the robot's human-like expression,the product deeply activates children's brains step by step according to the laws of language recognition thus making English-learning easier. It creates a pure English environment for children through the mechanism of emulational dialogue scene. With the function of oral pronunciation evaluation and learning outcome examination the product is dedicated to comprehensively enhancing children's English ability and thoroughly removing the problem of “Dumb English” for Chinese learners. It fully takes use of the AI recognition function, and intelligently selects teaching procedures and modules according to children's learning state and performance so as to increase their focus level and sense of fun.

Function highlights

New style of voice interaction,systematical way of learning; Obvious learning outcome,out of perfectly-made plans; Accurate spoken-English evaluation, less pronunciation mistakes; Robot imitating real dialogues, children enjoying scenario-based study; Intelligently recognizing inattention,attracting attention by amusement
